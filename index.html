About Vyom Logic AI

At Vyom Logic AI, our core philosophy revolves around harnessing the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence and robust backend systems to create tangible, positive impacts on human life. We believe technology should serve humanity, making processes more efficient, decisions more informed, and experiences more intuitive.

Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation, delivering solutions that are not just technically superior but also ethically sound and user-centric. We are committed to building a future where technology seamlessly integrates into our lives, empowering us to achieve more.

Chirag Trivedi

CEO & Founder

Chirag Trivedi is a seasoned Data Science professional with over 10 years of hands-on experience in building Gen AI applications and leading AI initiatives. As a Technical Lead specializing in Agentic AI, he has architected enterprise solutions including intelligent chatbots, enterprise search systems, and autonomous AI agents. His expertise spans Large Language Models (LLM), Natural Language Processing (NLP), RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and cutting-edge Agentic AI technologies. With a proven track record of leading teams and delivering production-ready AI solutions at leading organizations, Chirag founded Vyom Logic AI to democratize advanced AI capabilities and help businesses transform through intelligent automation.
